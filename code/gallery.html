History

Pamantasan ng Cabuyao, also known as Cabuyao University, is a higher education institution located in Cabuyao City, Laguna, Philippines. It was founded by the former Mayor Etok Aguillo, through the enactment of Municipal Ordinance 2003-059 approved on April 16, 2003.
